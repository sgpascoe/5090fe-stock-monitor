TWILIO SETUP - WAKE UP PHONE CALLS & SMS
=========================================

Twilio can make ACTUAL PHONE CALLS and send SMS that will wake you up even if DND is on!

SETUP STEPS:
------------

1. CREATE FREE TWILIO ACCOUNT:
   - Go to: https://www.twilio.com/try-twilio
   - Sign up (free tier includes $15.50 credit)
   - Verify your email and phone number

2. GET YOUR CREDENTIALS:
   - Go to: https://console.twilio.com/
   - Copy your Account SID
   - Copy your Auth Token (click to reveal)

3. GET A PHONE NUMBER:
   - Go to: Phone Numbers → Buy a Number
   - Choose a number (free with trial account)
   - This is your TWILIO_PHONE_NUMBER

4. ADD TO GITHUB SECRETS:
   - TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID = your Account SID
   - TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN = your Auth Token  
   - TWILIO_PHONE_NUMBER = your Twilio number (format: +1234567890)
   - ALERT_PHONE_NUMBER = YOUR phone number (format: +1234567890)
   
   For multiple phones (comma-separated):
   - ALERT_PHONE_NUMBERS = +1234567890,+0987654321

5. CONFIGURE ANDROID TO ALLOW CALLS THROUGH DND:
   - Settings → Sound → Do Not Disturb
   - Tap "Calls" → "Allow calls from"
   - Select "Contacts" or "All contacts"
   - Add your Twilio number to contacts (name it "RTX Alert" or similar)
   - OR: Settings → Apps → Phone → Notifications → Override Do Not Disturb: ON

6. CONFIGURE SMS TO BYPASS DND:
   - Settings → Apps → Messages → Notifications
   - Override Do Not Disturb: ON
   - High priority: ON
   - Add Twilio number to contacts

WHAT HAPPENS WHEN STOCK IS DETECTED:
-------------------------------------
✅ 3 PHONE CALLS to your number (5 seconds apart)
   - Each call rings for 30 seconds
   - Speaks: "Emergency alert. RTX 5090 graphics card is in stock now..."
   - This WILL wake you up even if DND is on!

✅ SMS with EMERGENCY prefix
   - Can bypass DND if configured
   - Sent immediately

✅ Plus all other alerts (Pushover, Telegram, etc.)
